About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the National University of Singapore, working with Professors Melvyn Sim and Napat Rujeerapaiboon. My research focuses on data-driven, human-preference-calibrated decision making under uncertainty, aiming at developing robust and distributionally robust decision making framework that are both theoretically sound and computationally tractable.

Research Interests

  • Methodologies: Robust and Distributionally Robust Optimization, Data-Driven Decision Making
  • Applications: Supply Chain Management, Humanitarian Logistics, Disaster Relief Operations
